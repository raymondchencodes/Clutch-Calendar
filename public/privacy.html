Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Clutch Calendar is a tool that helps users automatically add their university class schedule to Google Calendar. We care about your privacy and collect as little information as possible.

Information We Collect

  • When you use Google Sign-In, we request access to the Calendar Events scope (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events).
  • This permission allows the app to create events on your Google Calendar.
  • We do not collect, store, or access:
    • Your name
    • Your email
    • Your password
    • Your Google account information
    • Any existing calendar data beyond the events we generate

How Your Data Is Used

  • Your class schedule is temporarily stored in your browser session and on our server session only long enough to create events.
  • After the events are created, the schedule is immediately cleared from the server session.
  • No data is logged, saved, sold, or used for analytics or advertising.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

Clutch Calendar does not share, sell, transfer, or disclose any user data to any third party outside of Google services required for the application to function.

The only third party involved is Google, and the only data accessed is the permission required to create calendar events that you request. No data is shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or external services.

Data Protection & Security

We take data protection seriously. To safeguard your information:

  • All communication between Clutch Calendar and Google APIs is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • Temporary session data is stored only in memory and is automatically cleared after use.
  • No user data is stored in any database, file system, or persistent storage.
  • No sensitive information is ever logged on the server.

Because we do not retain any personal data after your session ends, all sensitive information remains under your control.

Third-Party Services

We use the Google Calendar API solely to create recurring events based on your class schedule. We do not modify, read, or access any other calendar events.

Data Retention

  • No personal data is stored after your session ends.
  • No user information is saved in any database.
