Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Clutch Calendar is a tool that helps users automatically add their university class schedule to Google Calendar. We care about your privacy and collect as little information as possible.

Information We Collect

  • When you use Google Sign-In, we request access to the Calendar Events scope (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar.events).
  • This permission allows the app to create events on your Google Calendar.
  • We do not collect or store:
    • Your name
    • Your email
    • Your password
    • Your Google account information
    • Any calendar data beyond the events we generate

How Your Data Is Used

  • Your schedule is temporarily stored in your browser session and on our server session only long enough to create events.
  • After the events are added, your schedule is cleared from the server session.
  • No data is saved, logged, sold, or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

We use the Google Calendar API solely to create recurring events based on your class schedule. We do not modify, access, or read any other calendar events.

Data Retention

  • No personal data is stored after your session ends.
  • No database is used to store user information.
